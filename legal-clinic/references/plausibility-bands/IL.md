# Plausibility bands — District Court, Hong Kong

**This file is a starting structure. Supervisor: fill in the typical ranges and citations before relying on `/legal-clinic:deadlines` plausibility checks for DC matters.** Until filled in, every entry the skill accepts carries `warnings: no-plausibility-band` and the check is effectively off.

Every citation here is `[model knowledge — verify]` unless replaced with a connector-retrieved or supervisor-provided source.

## How to use

- One row per deadline type the clinic sees regularly.
- Typical range is a plausibility window, not a holding.
- Cite the governing rule (RDC = Rules of the District Court, Cap 336H) in the Notes column so the student has somewhere to recompute against.
- Computation-of-time rules (RDC Ord 3) apply to every entry.
- DC civil limit: up to HK$3,000,000 (or unlimited in equity jurisdiction). DC criminal: most indictable offences except murder, manslaughter, and rape.

## Common deadline ranges — District Court

| Deadline type | Typical range | Governing rule | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| Appearance to Writ of Summons | 14 days | RDC Ord 12, r 3 | From service |
| Defence to Statement of Claim | 28 days | RDC Ord 18, r 2 | From time for appearance expires |
| Counterclaim | Same as defence period | RDC Ord 18, r 4 | Must be set out in defence or with leave |
| Reply | 14 days | RDC Ord 18, r 3 | From service of defence |
| Discovery | 14 days | RDC Ord 24, r 2 | From order |
| Summons for directions | 28 days | RDC Ord 25, r 1 | After close of pleadings |
| Notice of Appeal (from DC to CA) | 28 days | RDC Ord 58, r 1 | From date of judgment |
| Time for service of originating process | 12 months | RDC Ord 6, r 8 | Extendable |

## Computation-of-time notes (RDC Ord 3)

- Follows the same rules as the Court of First Instance (see `plausibility-bands/CA.md` for details on clear days, first-day exclusion, last-day rule, and public holidays).
- DC Practice Directions supplement the RDC where applicable.
