# HK National Security Law (hk-nsl-rules)

Plugin for Claude providing expert knowledge on the Hong Kong National Security Law (NSL), covering offences, court procedures, police powers, and professional obligations.

## Skills

- **offence-classifier** — Classify conduct under NSL offences (secession, subversion, terrorism, collusion)
- **bail-assessment** — Bail assessment under Art 42 (no presumption of bail)
- **trial-procedure** — NSL trial procedure guide (no jury, designated judges, Art 44–46)
- **art55-jurisdiction** — Art 55 vs Art 41 jurisdiction assessment
- **practitioner-guidance** — Law Society / Bar Association guidance on NSL practice
