# HK Employment Plugin

Hong Kong employment law under the Employment Ordinance (Cap 57), Employees' Compensation Ordinance (Cap 282), MPF Schemes Ordinance (Cap 485), and anti-discrimination ordinances (Cap 480, 487, 527, 602). Covers contract drafting, termination, discrimination, workplace injury, trade unions, and Labour Tribunal procedure.

## Skills

| Skill | Purpose |
|---|---|
| `/hk-employment:contract-review` | Review employment contracts for Cap 57 compliance |
| `/hk-employment:termination-calculator` | Calculate notice, severance, LSP, holiday pay |
| `/hk-employment:discrimination-screener` | Screen for discrimination claims under Cap 480/487/527/602 |
| `/hk-employment:workplace-injury` | Employees' compensation claim procedure under Cap 282 |
| `/hk-employment:restrictive-covenant` | Review non-compete/non-solicit under HK common law |

## Key Legislation

- **Employment Ordinance (Cap 57)** — contract, termination, leave, wages, severance, LSP, protection
- **Mandatory Provident Fund Schemes Ordinance (Cap 485)** — MPF contributions and offsetting
- **Employees' Compensation Ordinance (Cap 282)** — workplace injury claims
- **Sex Discrimination Ordinance (Cap 480)** — sex, marital status, pregnancy
- **Disability Discrimination Ordinance (Cap 487)** — disability discrimination
- **Family Status Discrimination Ordinance (Cap 527)** — family responsibilities
- **Race Discrimination Ordinance (Cap 602)** — race, colour, ethnic origin
- **Trade Union Ordinance (Cap 332)**
- **Limitation Ordinance (Cap 347)** — time limits for claims
- **Labour Tribunal Ordinance (Cap 25)** — LT procedure and jurisdiction

**Every output is a draft for solicitor or barrister review — not legal advice.**
