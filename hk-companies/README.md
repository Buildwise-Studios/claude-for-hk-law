# HK Companies Plugin

**Hong Kong company law — incorporation, directors' duties, shareholder remedies, annual compliance, and winding up under Cap 622 and Cap 32.**

This plugin provides specialised tools for company law practitioners working with Hong Kong corporate matters under the Companies Ordinance (Cap 622) and winding-up provisions (Cap 32).

## Quick Start

1. Install this plugin in your Claude Code environment
2. Configure your MCP server connections (optional — see `.mcp.json`)
3. Use the skills below to research and draft on company law matters

## Skills

- **[Incorporation](skills/incorporation/SKILL.md)** — Company formation, articles of association, company secretary, registered office
- **[Directors' Duties](skills/directors-duties/SKILL.md)** — Directors' fiduciary duties (common law + Cap 622), disclosure, conflicts, derivative actions
- **[Shareholder Remedies](skills/shareholder-remedies/SKILL.md)** — Unfair prejudice petition (s.724), derivative actions, winding up on just and equitable grounds
- **[Annual Compliance](skills/annual-compliance/SKILL.md)** — Annual return, financial statements, significant controllers register
- **[Winding Up Review](skills/winding-up-review/SKILL.md)** — Creditors' voluntary liquidation, members' voluntary liquidation, compulsory winding up

## MCP Servers
