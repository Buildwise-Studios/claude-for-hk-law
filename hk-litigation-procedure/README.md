# HK Litigation Procedure Plugin

Hong Kong civil litigation under the Rules of the High Court (Cap 4A) and District Court (Cap 336H). Covers case management, pleadings, discovery, summons, trial procedure, costs, appeals, and enforcement. Includes CFA, CA, CFI, DC procedure.

## Skills

| Skill | Purpose |
|---|---|
| `/hk-litigation-procedure:pleadings-review` | Review statement of claim, defence, reply for compliance with RHC |
| `/hk-litigation-procedure:discovery-prep` | Prepare discovery list, affidavit of documents |
| `/hk-litigation-procedure:costs-estimator` | Estimate costs on party-party or indemnity basis |
| `/hk-litigation-procedure:appeal-routes` | Determine appeal route from any HK court |
| `/hk-litigation-procedure:limitation-checker` | Check limitation periods under Cap 347 |
| `/hk-litigation-procedure:enforcement-guide` | Enforcement options (writ of execution, charging order, garnishee) |

## Key Legislation

- **Rules of the High Court (Cap 4A)** — CFI and CA procedure
- **Rules of the District Court (Cap 336H)** — DC procedure
- **Hong Kong Court of Final Appeal Rules (Cap 484A)** — CFA procedure
- **Evidence Ordinance (Cap 8)** — evidence, hearsay, privilege
- **Limitation Ordinance (Cap 347)** — time limits for claims
- **High Court Ordinance (Cap 4)** — CFI jurisdiction and powers
- **District Court Ordinance (Cap 336)** — DC jurisdiction and powers

**Every output is a draft for solicitor or barrister review — not legal advice.**
