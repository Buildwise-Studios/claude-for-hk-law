# Hong Kong Legal Citation Style

Hong Kong legal citation follows a **modified UK-style system** with HK-specific rules. It does **not** follow the US Bluebook.

---

## Legislation Citation

### Primary Legislation (Ordinances)

**Full citation (first reference):**
> Companies Ordinance (Cap 622, Laws of Hong Kong)

**Short form (subsequent references):**
> Companies Ordinance (Cap 622)

**OR simply:**
> Cap 622

### Section references
> Section 3(1) of the Companies Ordinance (Cap 622)

**Or in shortened form after full citation:**
> s 3(1) of Cap 622

### Multiple sections
> ss 3–5

### Subsidiary Legislation
> Companies (Disclosure of Information) Regulation (Cap 622A)

**Section within subsidiary legislation:**
> s 10(2) of Cap 622A

### Bills (not yet enacted)
> Companies (Amendment) Bill 2024

**With Bill number:**
> Companies (Amendment) Bill 2024, Bill No 12/2024

**BOH (Bills of HKSAR) citation:**
> BOH 1/2024

## Case Citation

### Pre-1997 Cases

Before 1 July 1997, Hong Kong law reports used the **HKLR** series.

**Format:** `[Year] Volume HKLR Page`

> [1995] 1 HKLR 1

> [1996] 2 HKLR 85

### Post-1997 Cases — Official Neutral Citations (Standard)

Since the early 2000s, Hong Kong courts adopted a **neutral citation system** using court-specific identifiers:

| Court | Identifier | Format | Example |
|---|---|---|
| **Court of Final Appeal** | HKCFA | [Year] HKCFA Page | [2024] HKCFA 1 |
| **Court of Appeal** | HKCA | [Year] HKCA Page | [2023] HKCA 100 |
| **Court of First Instance** | HKCFI | [Year] HKCFI Page | [2023] HKCFI 2000 |
| **District Court** | HKDC | [Year] HKDC Page | [2023] HKDC 500 |
| **Magistrates' Courts** | not formally assigned | — | — |

**Full example:**
> **Ubam v The Incorporated Owners of Brilliant Court** [2024] HKCFA 1

> **HKSAR v Lee Ming-cheung** [2023] HKCFI 2000

### Parallel Citations (preferred practice)

It is best practice to cite **both** the official neutral citation and the printed law report:

> [2023] HKCFA 1, [2023] 2 HKLRD 1

> [2019] HKCA 750, [2019] 5 HKC 355

### Unofficial but Common Report Series

| Abbreviation | Full Name | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **HKEC** | Hong Kong Electronic Cases | Widely used, available on HKLII; covers all courts |
| **HKC** | Hong Kong Cases | Print law report (now discontinued) |
| **HKLRD** | Hong Kong Law Reports & Digest | Current official print series |
| **HKCLC** | Hong Kong Company Law Cases | Specialist company law |
| **HKLRC** | Hong Kong Legal Reports & Citations | Older series |

**Example with HKEC:**
> [2023] HKCFI 2000, [2023] HKEC 100

### Party Names in Citation

**Civil cases:** Party names are italicised; "v" is used (not "vs" or "versus")

> *Ubam v Incorporated Owners of Brilliant Court* [2024] HKCFA 1

**Criminal cases:** The prosecution is usually "HKSAR" or "Secretary for Justice"

> *HKSAR v Wong Wai-hung* [2022] HKCFA 15

### Unreported Judgments

Where no reported version is available:
> *Chan Tai-man v Li Sau-ying* [2024] HKCFI 1 (unreported, 2 January 2024)

## Journal Articles

**Format:** Author, "Title" (Year) Volume Journal Name Page

> Michael Ramsden, "National Security and the Rule of Law in Hong Kong" (2020) 136 LQR 369

> Simon NM Young, "The Hong Kong National Security Law: A Commentary" (2021) 49 HKLJ 1

**Common HK law journals:**
- HKLJ — Hong Kong Law Journal
- HKLQR — Hong Kong Law Quarterly Review (historical)
- CLR — Common Law Review
- Asia Pacific Law Review

---

## Books

**Format:** Author, *Title* (edition, Publisher Year) Page

> Peter Wesley-Smith, *An Introduction to the Hong Kong Legal System* (3rd edn, OUP 1998) 45

> Michael Ramsden, *Hong Kong Law of Contract* (Thomson Reuters 2022) para 4-015

**Chapter in edited book:**
> Simon NM Young, "Hong Kong's Court of Final Appeal" in J Chan (ed), *The Judicial System of Hong Kong* (HKU Press 2021) 89

## Online Sources

### Legislation
> https://www.elegislation.gov.hk — official Hong Kong e-Legislation (HKeL)

**Cite as:**
> Companies Ordinance (Cap 622, Laws of Hong Kong), s 3(1), available at https://www.elegislation.gov.hk

### Case Law
> https://www.judiciary.hk — official Hong Kong Judiciary judgments database

> https://www.hklii.org — Hong Kong Legal Information Institute (free access)

**Cite as:**
> [2024] HKCFA 1, available at https://www.judiciary.hk

### Department of Justice
> https://www.doj.gov.hk — press releases, legal policy documents

> https://www.basiclaw.gov.hk — Basic Law text and resources

## Key Principles Summary

1. **Neutral citation** (court + year + sequential number) is the modern standard for HK cases
2. **Parallel citations** (official + HKEC) are preferred
3. **No Bluebook** — HK follows a modified UK-style (OSCOLA-like) system
4. **First reference** to an Ordinance uses the full name + Cap number; thereafter shortened
5. **Bilingual** — both Chinese and English citations are valid; English preferred for international audiences
6. **Judgments URL** — official source is judiciary.hk; HKLII for free access
7. **Legislation URL** — e-legislation.gov.hk is the authoritative online source

## Quick Reference Card

| Item | Format |
|---|---|
| Ordinance | *Ordinance Name* (Cap XXX, Laws of Hong Kong), s Y |
| Case (CFA) | *Name v Name* [Year] HKCFA Page |
| Case (CA) | *Name v Name* [Year] HKCA Page |
| Case (CFI) | *Name v Name* [Year] HKCFI Page |
| Case (DC) | *Name v Name* [Year] HKDC Page |
| Bill | Bill No XX/YYYY |
| Journal | Author, "Title" (Year) Vol Journal First Page |
| Book | Author, *Title* (edition, Publisher Year) Page |
| URL | https://www.elegislation.gov.hk |

> **Pro tip:** When citing HK cases in submissions, always check the latest version on https://www.judiciary.hk to ensure the neutral citation number is correct and the judgment is the final/relevant version.
